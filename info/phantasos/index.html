Changing your location:

Go to woeidlookup.com and get your "woeid"

Changing the background:

Open Filza, iFile, or any other file manager
Navigate to /var/mobile/Library/iWidgets
Open file style.css
In line that starts with "background-url" replace greenishblue.png to your chosen background from Gradients folder. The options are:

blueishblue
darkish
reenishblue
orangishred
pinkish
purpleish
purplishblue
redishred
yellowishgreen
yellowishorange

so that greenishblue.png becomes, for example, purpleish.png 

If you want the background to be empty (no image) enter any random word in place of greenishblue.png
